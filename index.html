Government Schemes

Explore various government schemes available for Ter village residents

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Direct income support to eligible farmer families into their bank accounts.

Go to PM-KISAN Portal

What you need

  • Aadhaar number linked with mobile for OTP
  • Bank account details (account no. & IFSC)
  • Basic land details as per records

Step-by-step Guidelines

  1. Open pmkisan.gov.in and go to Farmer Corner ‚Üí New Farmer Registration.
  2. Select your State/District, enter Aadhaar number and verify via OTP.
  3. Fill your name as per Aadhaar, bank details (account & IFSC), address and land details.
  4. Submit the form and note the registration/reference number.
  5. Complete eKYC from Farmer Corner (Aadhaar OTP) or visit a nearby CSC for biometric eKYC.
  6. Check Beneficiary Status under Farmer Corner to see approval and instalments.
  7. If details are incorrect/rejected, update using Edit Aadhaar Details or use Helpdesk/Grievance.

Ayushman Bharat - PM-JAY

Health insurance coverage for eligible families with cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals.

Go to PM-JAY Portal

What you need

  • Aadhaar and ration card (or any valid ID as accepted locally)
  • Mobile number for verification

Step-by-step Guidelines

  1. Open pmjay.gov.in and use ‚ÄòAm I Eligible‚Äô to check your family‚Äôs eligibility.
  2. If eligible, visit the nearest CSC/PM-JAY kiosk or empanelled hospital.
  3. Get your PM-JAY e-card generated after ID verification.
  4. Find empanelled hospitals on the portal and choose a suitable one.
  5. At the hospital helpdesk, show your e-card/Aadhaar; the hospital will request pre-authorization.
  6. Take cashless treatment; no payment needed at the counter for covered procedures.
  7. Keep all discharge/medical papers and track any queries on the portal.

Gharkul Yojana (PMAY-G)

Affordable housing scheme for rural poor families to build pucca houses with basic amenities.

Go to PMAY-G Portal

What you need

  • Aadhaar card linked with bank account
  • Ration card or SECC-2011 data inclusion
  • Land ownership details or consent
  • Mobile number for verification

Step-by-step Guidelines

  1. Check your name in SECC-2011 list or approach Gram Panchayat for verification.
  2. If eligible, submit Aadhaar and bank details at GP for registration.
  3. Your application will be uploaded on PMAY-G portal by officials.
  4. After sanction, first installment is credited to your bank account.
  5. Start construction of house, subsequent installments are released in phases.
  6. Upload geo-tagged photos of house construction progress via official app.
  7. On completion, final verification is done by GP/Block officials.
